Machine Intelligence Group
of Boston College

MIG is a small group of driven undergraduates interested in working on challenging, real-world AI/ML applications. Our goal is to foster a machine learning community on campus by working on tangible industry-related projects as well as cutting-edge academic machine learning research.

Current Projects
